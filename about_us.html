Jan C. Harrison, M.D. graduated from Duke University and then from New York University School of Medicine in 1985. She completed an internship and residency in Internal Medicine at John's Hopkins Hospital in Baltimore Maryland in 1988 and has been practicing medicine in Durham for thirty years. Dr. Harrison is Board Certified in Internal Medicine.

Dr. Jan Harrison has been married for thirty-seven years to Dr. J. Kevin Harrison, a prominent invasive cardiologist at Duke University. They have three children.

Dr. Harrison's interests include endurance running, triathlons, biking, backpacking, yoga, and nutrition.


Dr. Esther Poza graduated from Stanford University and the the University of Arizona School of Medicine in 1984. She completed her Internal Medicine Residency at UNC in 1987 and is Board Certified in Internal Medicine. She has been practicing medicine in Durham for over thirty years.

Dr. Poza is married to Dr. John Buckwalter, an anesthesiologist at Duke Regional Hospital. They have two children.

Dr. Poza's interests include hiking, quilting, and Functional Medicine.


Stephanie Farris is our Front-Office Manager. She schedules appointments and will check you in and out for your appointments. She collects payments and files your insurance for you. When you call our office, it is likely she will be the first person you will speak with. You will find Stephanie very knowledgeable and helpful.
