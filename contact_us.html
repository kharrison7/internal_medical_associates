First Visit

Thank you for choosing our office for all your medical care needs. To assist us with your care, please arrive at least 10 minutes early to register. Please complete registration forms prior to your appointment by printing them out from the forms section and choosing New Patient Packet and the appropriate Health History Form. Please bring the following items to your visit.

  • Your insurance card and a photo ID
  • Medications that you are currently taking
  • Previous Medical Records - you can print a Request for Medical Records Release form and fill this out and send to your previous provider in advance of your scheduled appointment


    • Appointments

    Patients are seen by appointment only. We make every effort to stay on schedule because we realize your time is as valuable as ours. If you cannot keep your scheduled appointment, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance. This allows us maximum flexibility in scheduling emergency patients. You may be charged for a missed appointment if you do not provide advance notification.



    Telephone Calls

    For non-emergency concerns and prescription refills, please call during regular office hours. Outside of office hours, our doctors respond to emergency calls on a rotating basis and our answering machine will provide you with the on-call physician's cell phone number. Please note, there is a charge for after hours calls.



    Request an Appointment

    If you would like to schedule an appointment, please call our office at (919) 471-5800. One of our staff will be happy to speak with you, schedule an appointment, and answer any questions that you may have.

    We at Internal Medical Associates look forward to seeing you and providing you with excellent comprehensive medical care for many years to come.
