Welcome to Internal Medicine Associates, where we serve patients from sixteen to over one hundred years old. We take care of the whole patient, providing health maintenance, wellness, and prevention, and comprehensive management of chronic medical problems such as hypertension, diabetes, thyroid disease, depression, and obesity. We make time to see you when you are ill or have been injured or simply are not feeling well.

Our staff is professional, well-trained, and compassionate, and we are all here to serve your medical needs.
