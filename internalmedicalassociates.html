Drs. Esther Poza and Jan Harrison, both board certified in Internal Medicine since 1985, have been practicing the art of medicine together for twenty-seven years.

Internal Medical Associates is a well established, respected private practice located in Durham, N.C., two blocks North of Duke Regional Hospital. As a physician-owned independent medical practice we answer to one person only, you, our patient. You are our only priority.


What Makes Internal Medical Associates Special.

1. We provide a comprhensive annual physical examination and wellness assessment we call you Health Maintenance Exam.

2. We provide well-woman care including pelvic exams, pap smears, and scheduling mammography.

3. We provide in office EKG and laboratory with excellent phlebotomists.

4. We can provide or if needed arrange screening for cancers, heart disease, vascular diseases, diabets, depression, and others.

5. We provide same day appointments if you call us in the morning.

6. You will always see a physician at Internal Medical Associates as we do not staff Nurse Practicioners or Physician Assistants.

7. Our staff answers all calls during office hours. We do Not have an automated phone system.

8. If you call the office after hous with an urgent problem you will be given Dr. Harrison's or Dr. Poza's cell phone number to call.

9. You will we receive prompt notification of all labwork, x-ray, and test results, and timely follow-up appointments with your physician.

10. We love to provide help with fitness and wellness, including Nutrition Counseling, Smoking Cessation, and Physical Activity, and Exercise Guidance.

11. When needed,we do our best to provide prompt referals to the best specialists. Being private, we can refer to any physician or facility you and your doctor choose.

12. We coordinate your medical care. We request and review notes from other physicians who have cared for you and from hospitalizations, and include these in your patient record and our plan of care for you
